Project Goals

Our Mission Statement

"To cultivate a compassionate community of crafty and creative crochet connoisseurs is our central concern"

Our end product will be a web application called "Stitch Central" where cochet and knitting enthusiasts can:

  1. Follow intuitive tutorials for beginners and experts alike
  2. Discuss current crochet and knitting trends in forums
  3. Ask and answer questions related to needle work

This project is scheduled to be completed by December 1, 2021
